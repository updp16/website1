Managing Director

Australian Business Education Centre (Pvt) Ltd is a professional education consultancy firm in Colombo, Sri Lanka providing excellent educational consultancy services related to higher education in world class institutions. We always strive to achieve best services to all our Customers.

Our focus is to exceed customer satisfaction in a student centred approach, while delivering quality students to our Foreign Principles. We dedicate all our experiences and resources professionally to ensure that students interested in pursuing higher education reach their highest potential in a smooth way, by providing them the reliable and most relevant information and guidance. Alternatively, we are dedicated to portray the partnering institutes accurately and in an ethical manner, while maintaining their quality standards.
